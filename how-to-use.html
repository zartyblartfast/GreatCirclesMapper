The Great Circle Mapper is designed to be intuitive and easy to use. Here are some simple steps to get you started:

  1. Select two countries: Use the dropdown boxes to for 'Airport A & B' to select the two countries you want to focus on.
  2. Search for the airports in the selected country: Enter a few characters to find the airport/city and select the airport from the list.
  3. Add the pair of airports to the Tag list: Click the 'Add' button after Airport A & B have been selected.
  4. Remove unwanted airport pairs: If you want to remove an airport pair, click the 'x' on the tag.
  5. Generate the map projections & Great Circles: Once you have selected your airport pairs, clik the Generate button.

Remember, The Great Circle Mapper is based on well established knowledge about great circles and how all 2D map projections involve distortions.
